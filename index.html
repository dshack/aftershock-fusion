Aftershock Fusion

June 6-8 2014

From the ashes of the Fusion Exchange and Atomic Blues Fusion, Aftershock will be a community-focused, affordable, and FUN dance weekend in San Francisco, CA.

Details

  • Local hosting and transit-accessible venue(s), with two full class tracks Saturday and Sunday.
  • Classes and Friday/Sunday evening dances will be held at the Women's Building, the first woman-owned and operated community center in the country. Late night + Saturday dance locations TBA.
  • Non-profit organization: any extra funds will be shared among instructors, DJs, and the community. Through affordable passes, volunteer opportunities, and scholarships, we're committed to making the event accessible to everyone.
  • Currently recruiting coordinators (local hosting, food, others), instructors, DJs, volunteers. Get in touch!

The Team
